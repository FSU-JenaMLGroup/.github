---

## 🔬 Research Areas

- 🪨 **Digital Rock Physics (DRP)** — pore-scale imaging, CT scanning, and deep learning-based upscaling of geological heterogeneity
- 🌡️ **Geothermal Reservoir Modelling** — AI-assisted simulation of permeability evolution and clogging in deep sandstone geothermal systems
- ☢️ **Geological Repositories for Radioactive Waste** — multiscale AI workflows to predict flow and transport properties in potential host rocks
- 🧪 **Porous Media & Reactive Transport** — coupling dissolution, mobilisation, and aggregation processes in synthetic and natural core materials
- 🤖 **Machine Learning for Geosciences** — CNNs, super resolution, image analysis, and data-driven methods for subsurface applications
